Career objective

To grow as a professional tailor and garment specialist by delivering reliable sewing, thoughtful customization, and consistent quality on every project whether it is a pair of pants, a school or company uniform, or a one of a kind outfit. I aim to build long term relationships with clients who value fit, durability, and honest workmanship.

What I do

Focused on textiles and construction: sewing, fitting, and finishing for clothes and pants.

  • Sewing & construction

    Seams, hems, linings, and clean finishing for garments that hold their shape.

  • Customization

    Adjustments and bespoke details so the piece fits your body and your style.

  • Uniforms

    School, office, or team uniforms made or altered to a consistent standard.

  • Pants & bottoms

    Tapering, waist and length changes, repairs, and new makes in your chosen fabric.
